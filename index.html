Visitor Lookup

Consolidates and presents site visitor information from multiple closed systems for easy access to all site staff.

This app is used as a launch page to other internal applications that access visitor information.

Can be used with a keyword lookup that matches the name, company, department or job title of the visitor. Or can be used by entering a date range to see all visitors in that time period.

  • Used By :All staff - particualrly Planners & Supervisors
  • Technology :Dotnet 8 / Blazor
  • Systems :INX InFlight, Gallagher, WorkDay
  • Access :Internal - authenticated users

Key Benefits

This application brings visitor data from INX InFlight, INX Intuition, Gallagher and Workday.

  • onsite times
  • flight and accomodation bookings
  • work group and site contact
  • induction and compliance status
  • emergency contact
  • past and future roster plan

"Before we had the Visitor Lookup app we had to constantly send emails to the Travel team to confirm our contractor bookings. This app was a huge producitivty boost for our supervisors."

Services, Superintendent

QR Compliance Check

Adds a QR code to all site access cards that links to the individuals compliance status.

This enables the ability for a live in-field spot checking of a persons compliance at any time.

The app can be used by external contractors as it's accessed directly over the internet.

  • Used By :All Site Workers
  • Technology :Dotnet 8
  • Systems :INX InTuition / Gallagher
  • Access :Internal - authenticated users

Overview

The app splits all compliances into 3 sections.

  • inductions - general site access and area inductions
  • role - compliances required for the nominated role specified against the visitor
  • individual - any addition competencies the individual has obtained

INX InTuition

The QR Compliance Check app simply renders information that has been correctly setup in INX InTuition. Considerable work was involved in assisting the business in structuring the sites compliance policy correctly in INX first.

"In November 2023 we had a large shutdown with over 700 contractors. The QR compliance checker allowed us to capture and verify that work was occurring in a compliant manner. The contractor supervisors able to do the checks themselves was a huge help!"

Utilities, Superintendent

Evacuation Mustering

Integrates with the Gallagher API to provide a live evacuation and mustering interface.

Allows members of the Incident Management Team the ability to closely monitor the progress of an evacuation and also to manually update personnel locations.

This is displayed on a large TV in the Incident Management control room.

  • Used By :IMT / Security
  • Technology :Dotnet 8
  • Systems :Gallagher
  • Access :Internal - Restricted

Features

The app splits all compliances into 3 sections.

  • Integrates directly with the Gallagher API
  • Live status of which zones all personnel are in
  • Simple interface to filter by zones to identify priority personnel to evacuate
  • Live feed of personnel zone changes
  • Bulk changing of perssonnel zones
